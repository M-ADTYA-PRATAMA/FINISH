JETT

Jett. Representing his native South Korea, Jett's agile fighting style with plenty of evasion allows him to take risks that others can't. He circled around each battle, cutting down the enemy before they realized what was happening.

NEON

The agent from the Philippines, Neon, shot forward at astonishing speed, spitting out bioelectric beams as fast as his body produced them. He ran to surprise the enemy, then attacked them faster than lightning.

REYNA

Forged in the heart of Mexico, Reyna dominates single combat and becomes stronger with each kill. His prowess is only hampered by his raw skill, making him rely heavily on performance.

OMEN

A ghost in the mind, Omen hunts in the shadows. He blinds the enemy, teleports across the battlefield, then lets his paranoia haunt him as the enemy tries to guess where he will attack next.
